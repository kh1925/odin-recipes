Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan.
  2. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties on the grill for about 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Add cheese slices on top of patties during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast burger buns on the grill until golden brown.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, and mustard.
