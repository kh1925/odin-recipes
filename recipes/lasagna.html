Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until soft.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
