Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine made from wheat flour and water, typically shaped into various forms and cooked in boiling water.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until softened.
  5. Drain pasta and add it to the skillet with the tomato mixture.
  6. Toss with fresh basil, salt, and pepper.
  7. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
